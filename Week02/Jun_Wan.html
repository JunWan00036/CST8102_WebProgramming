Kingston is a Canadian city on Lake Ontario, at the mouth of the Cataraqui and St. Lawrence rivers.
It's known as the " Limestone City " for its grand 19th-century buildings,
including the lakeside Kingston City Hall . The grand house and gardens of
the Bellevue House National Historic Site commemorate Canada's first prime minister,
Sir John A. Macdonald . Fort Henry , built in the 1800s, holds military demonstrations.
